My Favourites

Formula 1

Formula One, commonly known as Formula 1 or F1, is the highest class of international racing for open-wheel single-seater formula racing cars sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile(FIA). The race gives the audience and the team a thrilling, intense, and nerve-wracking experience, making it one of my favourite things to watch over the weekends.

Main reasons why I love the sport:

  • The Thrill
  • The Intensity
  • The Speed
  • The high-stakes involved
  • The Adrenaline Rush
